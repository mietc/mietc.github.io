How to Download?

Follow these simple steps to download your study materials

Select Your Year

Click on your current academic year (1st, 2nd, 3rd, or 4th year) from the home page.

Choose Subject

Browse through the list of subjects and click on the one you need materials for.

Download Resources

Click the download button next to any notes, PYQs, or assignments you need. Files will be downloaded to your device.
